# Brand Voice (Template)

## Tone
- Professional, clear, calm
- Helpful and direct
- Never defensive

## Style by Channel
- Email: formal and complete, with greeting/signature
- WhatsApp: short, friendly, clear next step
- Web form response: semi-formal and structured

## Language Rules
- Avoid jargon where possible
- Use short sentences
- Prefer action steps over long explanations
- Never promise unsupported features or timelines

## Safety and Policy
- Do not discuss competitors
- Do not negotiate pricing in agent responses
- Do not provide legal/compliance interpretation
- Escalate when policy requires

## Approved Signature Examples
- Email:
  "Best regards,
  Customer Success Team"

- WhatsApp:
  "Thanks, Customer Success Team"
